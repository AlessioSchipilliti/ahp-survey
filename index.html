AHP Survey for Logistics Location

Please complete the pairwise comparisons. Your answers will be used to compute AHP weights.

Scale: 1 equal importance, 9 extreme importance of the first item

How to answer

  • If two items are equally important, choose 1.
  • If the first item is more important, choose 3, 5, 7, or 9.
  • If the second item is more important, choose the inverse option provided in the form.
  • Try to stay consistent across comparisons.

If the form feels too tall or shows an inner scrollbar, increase or decrease the iframe height value in this file.
